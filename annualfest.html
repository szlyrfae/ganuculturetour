    RXZ Members Terengganu refers to the community of Yamaha RXZ motorcycle enthusiasts and owners in Terengganu who organize and participate in large-scale motoring gatherings, such as the annual "RXZ Members" event which is famous throughout Malaysia, becoming a symbol of the state's unique heritage which combines motoring activities with family spirit, community activities (such as free food distribution), and non-political side events, attracting thousands of participants from all over the country to Terengganu.

    The Squid Jigging Festival in Terengganu is an annual event that celebrates the traditional activity of "squid jigging", where anglers use colorful jigs to attract squid at night, making it a major tourist attraction that promotes marine culture, the local economy, and offers tourists a unique experience of catching squid from a boat or beach during the peak season (April-August).

    The Terengganu Beach Sports Festival is an annual event organised by the Terengganu State Sports Council (MSNT) Facebook page (MSNT) to promote beach sports, recreation and culture of Terengganu, usually held at Teluk Ketapang Beach, Kuala Nerus, featuring sports such as beach football, volleyball, sepak takraw, and other activities such as fishing and sports, open to all ages, said the Terengganu State Sports Council (MSNT) Facebook page (MSNT) and Discovery Terengganu.

    The Asean Monsoon Casting Tournament (AMCT) in Terengganu, Malaysia, is an annual international angling competition held at Tasik Kenyir (Kenyir Lake) during the monsoon season, attracting anglers worldwide to fish for species like giant snakehead in Southeast Asia's largest man-made lake, promoting tourism and local culture alongside the fishing event. It's a significant event for sportfishing enthusiasts, featuring professional anglers and boosting the regional economy, organized by the Terengganu State Government with support from bodies like Tourism Malaysia and KETENGAH (Terengganu Tengah Development Board).
